LONDON

London, the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom, is a vibrant, multicultural metropolis with a rich history, serving as a global center for business, finance, and culture, and a popular tourist destination.

NETHERLAND

The Netherlands, often informally called Holland, is a low-lying, densely populated country in Northwestern Europe, known for its canals, windmills, and vibrant cities, with a rich history and a strong focus on water management.

INDIA

India, officially the Republic of India, is a vast and diverse country in South Asia, known for its rich history, diverse cultures, and vibrant traditions, encompassing a wide range of landscapes from the Himalayas to the Indian Ocean.

NEW YORK

New York is a global center of finance and commerce, culture, technology, entertainment and media, academics and scientific output, the arts and fashion, and, as home to the headquarters of the United Nations, international diplomacy.

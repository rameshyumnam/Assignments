Omlet

Ingredients list

  • 2 Eggs
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Oil

Recipies

  1. Pour the egg out in a bowl.
  2. Chop the onion into small peaces.
  3. Mix onion, eggs and half tablespoon of salt, stir well.
  4. Put 2 tablespoon of oil on a heated pan.
  5. When the oil is hot put the mixed eggs into it.
  6. Fry for 5 minutes.
  7. Ready to eat.

Rice

Ingredients list

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Cardamom

Recipies

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Wash the rice with water.
  3. Put it into the boiled water.
  4. Boil for 5mins. And steam for 15mins.
  5. Ready to eat.

Tea

Ingredients list

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Tea Leaves

Recipies

  1. Boil water upto 100 degrees.
  2. When boiling add sugar and let it boil for 2 minutes.
  3. After that put some tea leaves and boil for another 2 minutes.
  4. Ready to serve.
